About SQC

The Science & Technology Club (SQC) is a dedicated community of innovators and inventors at NIT Jalandhar, committed to exploring cutting-edge technologies and scientific advancements.

We believe in the power of innovation to transform the world. Through projects, workshops, and collaborations, we foster a culture of creativity and technical excellence.
